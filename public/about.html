About Hawthorne Staffing

Hawthorne Staffing is a boutique solution provider for Tech Talent and Project Management recruitment and staff augmentation.

Having over 20 years of hands-on IT experience in numerous industries, its founder Maya Hawthorne, a certified Project Management Professional (PMI), has managed a spectrum of Infrastructures spanning from small scale to global networks, that include both hardware management and deployment, as well as software project management and development.

She has led teams that are diverse and cross-functional, from local desktop techs to international enterprise solution/network engineers and architects – having gained a systemic and holistic understanding of how businesses operate from startup to Fortune 10 levels.

Her diverse background also includes an educational foundation in Transportation, Supply Chain, and Logistics Management, as well as broad experience in Telecom, Manufacturing, and Academia – a natural fit to support large organizations with a portfolio of agencies/subsidiaries.

Recognizing that a cleaner climate transition implementation and upgrade requires a material amount of work effort and cross-functional leadership knowledge, Maya's legacy focus is now on searching and training for the next generation workforce to build these resilient, smart, and sustainable infrastructures.
